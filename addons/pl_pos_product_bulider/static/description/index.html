POS Product Warehouse Quantity

Do you want to display warehouse or locations product stock in the POS? Your warehouse and warehouse locations play an important role in product sales. You have to make sure that the ordered goods are in stock. This module helps to display the available stock quantity of all products in the POS. Here you get the total product quantity and how much quantity available in each warehouse or location.

In the point of sale tick "Display Warehouse Stock" to display warehouse product stock and choose warehouse for display stock by warehouse.


Click the smart button that given to the below of products to see the stock quantity.


After pressing on the smart button, the popup box opens that shows all stock details of that product with warehouse product quantity.


You can check product stock details when your product is on the cart.


The popup box opens with all the stock details of the product.

In the point of sale tick "Display Warehouse Stock" to display warehouse product stock and choose location for display stock by location.


Click the smart button that given below of products to see the stock quantity and You can check product stock details when your product is on the cart.


After that, the popup box opens that shows all stock details of that product with the location.

You can set the minimum quantity from the configuration. Select location to quantity check and order.


It displays the available product quantity for the selected warehouse.


Warning popup generates if you order more than the minimum quantity.
